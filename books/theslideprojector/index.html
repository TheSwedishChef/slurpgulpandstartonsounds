From the back cover…

This chapbook on The Slide Projector is an extract from BLOCK, a poetry anthology by Chris Lorensson.

A chocolate cake crumbles, streaking fat across red beams and leaving a room in messy decadence. Beneath the laughter lies a question of gluttony and greed—but also one of self-discovery and ultimately, self-acceptance.

The Slide Projector is a personal and cultural mirror, daring to reflect our consumption back at us while mining for new creative paths that lay buried deep beneath roads we already walk
