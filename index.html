Slurp, Gulp and Start on Sounds

Poetry by Chris Lorensson

Podcast

I’ve been writing poetry since the mid-90’s. I put together a book called Slurp, Gulp and Start on Sounds, but then my hard drive crashed and I lost the layout files before I could go to print. I have one proof copy that I read from for the podcast of the same name.
